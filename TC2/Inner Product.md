The inner product operator of two signals is defined as:$$\langle s_1, s_2 \rangle = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}s_1(t)s_2^*(t)\,dt$$where $s^*(t)$ is the complex conjucate of $s(t)$. It is a metric of similarity of the two signals+++.

properties:
- $\langle s_1, s_2 \rangle = \langle s_2, s_1 \rangle ^*$ 
- $\langle s_1+s_2, s_3 \rangle = \langle s_1, s_3 \rangle + \langle s_2, s_3 \rangle$
- $\langle as_1,s_2 \rangle = a\langle s_1,s_2 \rangle$
